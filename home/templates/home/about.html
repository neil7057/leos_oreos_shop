Leo's Emporium

Hi, and welcome to Leo's Oreo Emporium. A site dedicated to the sale of all things Oreo.

The Leo in the title is my son who is on the Autism spectrum and one of his passions is oreo and everything oreo - he has quite a collection. He's particularly passionate about Blackpink

All the products on the site are genuine and available elsewhere.


Sadly, the products arent actually for sale on this site as this is my City of Bristol Full stack Development Milestone Project 4 (MP4).

Training was provided remotely from the Code Institute which is based in Dublin, Ireland.

It's a fully working E-commerce store with product managemnet and payment processing built with a python django framework using HTML5, CSS3, Javascript/Jquery, sqllite. User managemnet is django allauth and payment processing uses Stripe.
